Cake Scaling Calculator

Calculator Description and Instructions

This calculator is great for scaling cakes from your favorite recipes. This tool has saved me a ton of time when baking custom 3-tier cakes

Tips

  • Generally you do not need to adjust cooking time, but you should still test with a toothpick.
  • To save time, you can copy and paste an ingredient list into a AI chatbot along with instructions to create a python dictionary out of it. Just make sure to read over the dictionary and confirm its accuracy before baking!
  • If you need to bake the cake in batches, some batter will not be able to hold up if made at the beginning.
